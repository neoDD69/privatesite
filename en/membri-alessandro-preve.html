Alessandro Preve

Architetto
Architect specializing in exclusive environments and green homes design

I am someone who is never satisfied and always needs new stimuli, seeking to engage with the people and life around me to continue growing. Thanks to my continuous curiosity and a couple of "special" people, I found my way to the world of Private Community®.

A year later, when I look in the mirror, I see a different, better person—confident, aware of my abilities, and able to always find solutions and answers to the questions posed by both professional and personal life. I build new relationships, always centering the values I believe in.

Frequenting these positive environments not only benefits and nourishes my "self," but has also allowed me to acquire a more open and visionary mindset for a better life and to be ready for challenges without ever neglecting emotions.

Today, I have a defined vision and mission, have clearly identified my target market, and am opening up to new growth opportunities that I could not have imagined until recently. In conclusion, Private Community® is the environment where I want to continue to take my steps.
