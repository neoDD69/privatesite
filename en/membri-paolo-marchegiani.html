Paolo Marchegiani

Elettro 2000 TLC Srl
Electrical systems, distribution, power, lighting, and special installations

Personally, my experience with Private Community® began out of curiosity and a desire to consolidate my working methods and personal strategies. The Community later became fundamental and has been immensely beneficial because I was able to refine some of my skills, study, and finally write my Vision and Mission!

A key role is played by appointments such as the weekly Labs, which, through discussion and dialogue, can immediately solve problems that may seem difficult to us but are simple for others. Additionally, the training I have accessed, particularly the Connection Booster course, has greatly helped me to discover people's character traits as soon as I come into contact with them, which is incredibly important for an entrepreneur.

P.S. The added value for my decision? The community managers, whom I consider special people.
