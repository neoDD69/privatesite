Antonio Dell’Aversana

Studio Legale Dell'Aversana
Lawyer specializing in family law and expert in preventive business consultancy

Initially, my decision to join Private Community® was driven by the complete trust I have in the people who introduced it to me; in fact, I had strong doubts about the effectiveness of such a formula for a law firm.

I had to change my mind. Here, I found a way to transform my personal life and, consequently, my professional life as well. Thanks to Private Community®, I became aware of why I do what I do and thus can now focus my energies only on what aligns with me. Besides the initial courses, the most useful tool is undoubtedly the Community with its monthly meetings and the opportunity to have a table where one can discuss their problems and goals with people who are aligned in terms of values. Thanks to the Community and the advice and collaboration of its members, my vision, which was set over a five-year period, was realized in just over a year. From being a lawyer with a room in another colleague's office, in a year and a half, I managed to build a practice with eight collaborators, allowing me to enjoy a better quality of life.
